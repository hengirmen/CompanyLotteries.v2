How It Works

  1. Purchase tickets during the Purchase Phase
  2. Keep your random number secret until the Reveal Phase
  3. Reveal your number during the Reveal Phase
  4. Winners are selected automatically after the Reveal Phase ends

Important Rules

  • You must reveal your random number during the Reveal Phase to be eligible to win
  • Each ticket requires a unique random number
  • The lottery will only proceed if the minimum ticket requirement is met
  • If minimum requirements are not met, all participants can claim refunds

Phases

1. Purchase Phase

During this phase, participants can buy tickets. Each ticket requires:

  • Payment in the specified token
  • A secret random number (keep it safe!)

2. Reveal Phase

During this phase, participants must:

  • Reveal their random numbers
  • Verify their ticket ownership

Note: Failure to reveal your random number during the Reveal Phase will result in ticket invalidation.

Winner Selection

Winners are selected through a verifiable random process that combines:

  • Participant-provided random numbers
  • Blockchain-based randomness
  • Timestamp data

This lottery is governed by smart contracts on the blockchain. All operations are transparent and verifiable.
